## ARTICLE III — MANAGEMENT AND OPERATIONS

**3.1 Management by Member.** The Company shall be managed exclusively by the Member. The Member shall have full, exclusive, and complete authority to manage and control the business affairs of the Company.

**3.2 Powers.** The Member shall have the power to:
- (a) Enter into contracts and agreements on behalf of the Company;
- (b) Open and maintain bank accounts;
- (c) Hire employees and independent contractors;
- (d) Purchase, sell, lease, or otherwise deal with property;
- (e) Borrow money and incur obligations;
- (f) Bring and defend lawsuits;
- (g) Take any other action necessary or desirable for the conduct of the Company's business.

**3.3 Banking.** The Company shall maintain its funds in accounts at financial institutions designated by the Member. Only the Member (or authorized signers designated by the Member) may draw on Company accounts.

**3.4 Company Records.** The Company shall maintain at its principal office:
- (a) A current list of the full name and last known business, residence, or mailing address of the Member;
- (b) A copy of the Articles of Organization and all amendments thereto;
- (c) Copies of the Company's federal, state, and local tax returns for the three most recent years;
- (d) Copies of this Operating Agreement and all amendments thereto;
- (e) Financial statements for the three most recent years.

## ARTICLE IV — ALLOCATIONS AND DISTRIBUTIONS

**4.1 Profits and Losses.** All profits and losses of the Company shall be allocated to the Member.

**4.2 Distributions.** The Member may make distributions from the Company at any time and in any amount, subject to applicable law regarding fraudulent transfers and insolvency.

**4.3 Tax Treatment.** The Company shall be treated as a disregarded entity for federal income tax purposes. All income, deductions, credits, and other tax items shall be reported on the Member's personal tax return.

## ARTICLE V — TRANSFER OF MEMBERSHIP INTEREST

**5.1 Transfers.** The Member may freely transfer, sell, assign, or otherwise dispose of all or any portion of the Member's interest in the Company.

**5.2 Admission of New Members.** Additional members may be admitted to the Company only with the written consent of the Member. Upon admission of any additional member, this Agreement shall be amended to reflect the terms of a multi-member operating agreement.

## ARTICLE VI — DISSOLUTION AND WINDING UP

**6.1 Dissolution Events.** The Company shall be dissolved upon the earliest to occur of:
- (a) A written decision by the Member to dissolve the Company;
- (b) The death, incapacity, or bankruptcy of the Member (unless a successor is designated);
- (c) Entry of a decree of judicial dissolution;
- (d) Any event that makes it unlawful for the Company's business to continue.

**6.2 Successor Member.** Upon the death or incapacity of the Member, the following person(s) shall succeed to the Member's interest: {{successor_name}} ({{successor_relationship}}).

**6.3 Winding Up.** Upon dissolution, the Member (or the successor or a person appointed by a court) shall wind up the Company's affairs by:
- (a) Collecting all debts owed to the Company;
- (b) Paying all Company debts and obligations;
- (c) Distributing remaining assets to the Member (or successor).

**6.4 Articles of Dissolution.** Upon completion of winding up, Articles of Dissolution (or equivalent) shall be filed with the {{filing_office}}.

## ARTICLE VII — LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION

**7.1 Limited Liability.** The Member shall not be personally liable for any debts, obligations, or liabilities of the Company, whether arising in contract, tort, or otherwise, solely by reason of being a member of the Company.

**7.2 Indemnification.** The Company shall indemnify and hold harmless the Member and any agent of the Company from and against any and all claims, demands, losses, costs, expenses, damages, or liabilities arising out of or related to the Company's business, to the fullest extent permitted by law.

## ARTICLE VIII — GENERAL PROVISIONS

**8.1 Governing Law.** This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of {{state_name}}.

**8.2 Amendments.** This Agreement may be amended only by a written instrument executed by the Member.

**8.3 Severability.** If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

**8.4 Entire Agreement.** This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior agreements and understandings.

**8.5 Headings.** The headings in this Agreement are for convenience only and shall not affect the interpretation of any provision.
