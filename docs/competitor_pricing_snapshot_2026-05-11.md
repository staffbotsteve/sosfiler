# Competitor Pricing Snapshot - May 11, 2026

Purpose: support SOSFiler's public pricing comparison with specific, reviewable numbers. This is product comparison research only; official government fees still come from state/county sources.

## Defensible Public Claim

Use this claim:

> SOSFiler is $29 plus official state fees to start an LLC with filing preparation, operating-agreement prep, and EIN filing included.

Do not use this broader claim without qualification:

> Cheapest state filing fees.

Reason: official state filing fees are government pass-through fees. Several competitors advertise $0 formation packages, so the strongest accurate comparison is the exact service basket most founders need to start: state filing service, operating agreement, EIN filing, and then first annual filing service.
