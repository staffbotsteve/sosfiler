# State Formation Companion Fee Audit (2026)

Purpose: identify state-required documents, reports, notices, licenses, or state charges beyond the base formation filing for domestic entities. Use this to decide what SOSFiler must include in checkout pricing versus what must be disclosed as a post-formation obligation.

Scope: domestic LLCs, domestic business corporations, domestic nonprofits, and notable alternate entity types where the state fee schedule differs. County, city, newspaper, and tax-agency charges are listed as variable/disclosure items unless the state collects them as part of the formation package.

Legend:
- Include in quote: required state charge SOSFiler should collect or route as part of formation or immediate handled post-formation work.
- Disclose/track: required obligation but not a state formation charge collected at checkout, or amount is variable/tax-specific.

## Quote-Affecting Follow-Ups

High-confidence formation-package changes:

- California LLC already updated from $70 to $90 to include the $20 initial Statement of Information.
- South Carolina corporations should include the CL-1 / $25 initial corporate license fee when SOSFiler supports corporation formation there.
- Nevada corporations should include the initial list and business-license package rather than only the Articles fee.

Policy or workflow decisions before changing checkout totals:

- Alabama name reservation, Georgia initial annual registration, Louisiana supplemental initial report, New York certificate of publication, West Virginia business registration certificate, and publication states should be charged only if SOSFiler will actually perform those filings as part of the formation package.
- Annual reports, biennial reports, franchise taxes, annual taxes, and license taxes should be disclosed and tracked, but not rolled into first checkout unless SOSFiler is selling the first annual filing or tax-registration service.
- Corporation fees in share-based states need the authorized-share count before the final state pass-through can be quoted.
