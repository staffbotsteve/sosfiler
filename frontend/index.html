Frequently Asked Questions

Is $49 really the total price?
Yes. $49 covers everything: Articles of Organization, Operating Agreement, EIN filing, Registered Agent for the first year, compliance calendar, initial resolutions, meeting minutes, and membership certificates. The only additional cost is your state's filing fee, which goes directly to the Secretary of State (we don't mark it up).
What are the ongoing costs?
Registered Agent renewal is $49/year — flat, never increases. Annual report filing service is $25/year. Both are completely optional. No auto-renewals — we'll email you when renewal is coming up and you choose whether to continue.
How fast is the filing?
We prepare your filing immediately and submit through the fastest verified channel available for your state. Some portals support smooth automation; others require operator verification, CAPTCHA, or account steps. Your dashboard shows evidence-backed status updates instead of guesswork.
Do I need a lawyer?
For a standard LLC formation, typically no. Our documents are based on state-specific legal templates and are customized with your information. However, if you have complex ownership structures, intellectual property concerns, or unusual business arrangements, we recommend consulting with an attorney.
What state should I form my LLC in?
Usually your home state — where you live and do business. While Delaware, Wyoming, and Nevada are popular for certain situations, forming in a different state means you'll also need to register as a "foreign LLC" in your home state (extra cost and paperwork). Our wizard helps you make the right choice.
What's a registered agent?
Every LLC must have a registered agent — a person or service authorized to receive legal documents and government notices on behalf of your business. They must have a physical address in your state of formation. We serve as your registered agent, so you don't have to use your home address.
Can I cancel or get a refund?
If we haven't filed your documents yet, you can cancel for a full refund. After filing, we can't refund the state fee (it's already paid to the state), but we'll refund our $49 service fee if you're not satisfied. No questions asked.
How is this so cheap? What's the catch?
No catch. Our software maps your details directly to state-required forms and files them accurately. Other companies charge $300-600 because they have massive marketing budgets, call centers, and aggressive upsell funnels. We automated the paperwork and cut out the overhead. You get the same result for $49.
