# SOSFiler Brand Voice

## The Core Identity
We are the **ANTI-LegalZoom**. We are the antidote to the subscription traps, hidden fees, and "free" tiers that cost $300 by checkout. 

## Tone & Personality
- **Direct & Confident:** We say what we mean. No fluff. 
- **Honest & Transparent:** We show the exact price upfront. 
- **Trustworthy:** We don't use fear tactics to sell compliance packages. 
- **Not Corporate. Not Cute. Not Salesy:** We are a utility. We file your paperwork accurately and get out of your way.

## Pronouns & Perspective
- We say **"we"**, never "our team of experts" or "our legal professionals." 
- We are a tool, a software layer, and a service—not a law firm.

## The Forbidden Words
Never use these words in SOSFiler copy:
- Solutions
- Leverage
- Empower
- Seamless
- Cutting-edge
- Innovative
- Synergize
- "Peace of mind" (used by every competitor to sell fear)

## Required Elements & Style
- **Real numbers:** Always use exact figures (e.g., "$49 flat", "takes 5 minutes").
- **Plain English:** No legal jargon unless explicitly defining a necessary state term.
- **Direct comparisons:** We name the problem (the "$0 LLC" scam) and show how we differ.

## The Key Message
**"$49. Everything included. No hidden fees. No upsells. No auto-renewals."**
