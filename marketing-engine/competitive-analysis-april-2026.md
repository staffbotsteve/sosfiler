## Pricing: The SOSFiler Advantage

The industry runs on a "Free to Start, Expensive to Keep" model. SOSFiler destroys this.

### 5-Year Total Cost of Ownership

- **LegalZoom:** $0 (Year 1) + $249/yr RA + $99/yr AR = ~$1,392
- **SOSFiler:** $49 (Year 1) + $49/yr RA + $25/yr AR = **$345**
- **SOSFiler is ~$1,000 cheaper over 5 years.**

Closest ideological competitor: **Northwest** ($39 formation, anti-upsell stance) — but SOSFiler RA is 60% cheaper ($49 vs $125/yr).
