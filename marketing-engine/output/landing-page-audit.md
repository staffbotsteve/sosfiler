# SOSFiler Landing Page Copy Audit

## Audit Overview
The current landing page (https://sosfiler.com) was reviewed against the brand voice, writing rules, and competitive positioning. 

### Overall Score
**Grade: C+**
While the page mentions the $49 price point and "no hidden fees", it uses generic corporate fluff and violates several of the brand's forbidden rules (e.g., using "Everything your business needs," acting like a full-service agency instead of a precision software utility). It also heavily features a $49/yr Registered Agent renewal as "included," which contradicts our strict "No auto-renewals" and "$49 everything included" core message.

---

## Section-by-Section Analysis & Improvements

### 1. Hero Section
**Current Copy:**
"Every state filing. Done in minutes. From $49. LLCs, Corps, Nonprofits, DBAs, & Licenses. Everything your business needs. Simple formation packages starting at $49 for LLCs, plus full-service DBA filings, state business licenses, and registered agent services across all 50 states."

**Audit Feedback:**
- **Critique:** Too wordy. Dilutes the core message by mentioning DBAs and licenses upfront. "From $49" and "Packages starting at" sounds like the exact bait-and-switch we are trying to fight. 
- **Rule Violation:** We are supposed to say "$49 flat", not "From $49".

**Suggested Improvement (Before/After):**
- *Before:* Every state filing. Done in minutes. From $49.
- *After:* **Form Your LLC for $49 Flat. No Hidden Fees. No Subscriptions.**
- *Subheadline After:* We file your Articles of Organization, retrieve your EIN, and draft your Operating Agreement. Exactly $49. We file your paperwork accurately and get out of your way.

---

### 2. "Everything You Need. Nothing You Don't."
**Current Copy:**
Lists 6 items: Articles of Organization, EIN, Operating Agreement, Registered Agent (1 Year), Compliance Calendar, Complete Document Package.

**Audit Feedback:**
- **Critique:** The voice guide strictly says: "The 'What's Included' Trio: Always explicitly list Formation, EIN, and Operating Agreement." Adding "Complete Document Package" sounds like generic marketing fluff.
- **Rule Violation:** The page states "Registered Agent Renewal: $49/yr flat, forever." The brand guide says: "We don't automatically enroll you in registered agent services... When you pay $49, you are done paying us." If we include 1 year and then renew it, it's a subscription trap. If the product *does* do this, the positioning document is at odds with the product, but assuming the brand guide is the bible: we must remove the Registered Agent subscription trap language or clarify it does NOT auto-renew.

**Suggested Improvement:**
Highlight the Trio clearly.
- **Formation:** Your state paperwork filed accurately.
- **EIN:** We retrieve your tax ID from the IRS.
- **Operating Agreement:** A custom agreement to make your business bank-ready.

---

### 3. Comparison Table
**Current Copy:**
Shows SOSFiler vs LegalZoom, ZenBusiness, Northwest. 

**Audit Feedback:**
- **Critique:** The table is actually quite good and aligns with the competitive analysis. It uses real numbers. However, it still references a Registered Agent renewal. 
- **Improvement:** Ensure the "Real Total (First Year)" is bolded. Emphasize that competitors charge $70-$99 for the EIN, whereas it is included in our $49.

---

### 4. How It Works
**Current Copy:**
"Three steps. That's it. 1. Enter Your Details (Our wizard guides you through everything) 2. We Generate & File (instantly generate all your formation documents) 3. Track in Real-Time."

**Audit Feedback:**
- **Critique:** Clear and concise, fits the utility tone. 

---

### 5. Frequently Asked Questions
**Current Copy:**
"How is this so cheap? What's the catch? No catch. We automated the entire process — AI-generated documents..."

**Audit Feedback:**
- **Critique:** "AI-generated documents" can scare users who want precision and accuracy for state legal forms. State forms are standardized templates, not ChatGPT prompts. 
- **Suggested Improvement:** 
- *Before:* We automated the entire process — AI-generated documents, automated state filing...
- *After:* We automated the entire process. Our software maps your details directly to the exact state-required forms. Other companies charge $300 because they have massive marketing budgets and aggressive upsell funnels. We just form your LLC.

---

### 6. Missing Elements & Trust Signals
- **Social Proof:** The page lacks specific, numerical proof or realistic quotes from the defined ICPs (First-time entrepreneur, Serial entrepreneur). 
- **Actionable CTA:** "Start My LLC for $49 →" is decent, but "Form My LLC for $49 Flat" is stronger. 
- **Forbidden Words:** The current page is mostly clean of the forbidden words, but the tone leans slightly into "full-service provider" rather than "precision software utility."

## Summary of Action Items for Web Team:
1. Change "From $49" to "$49 Flat" everywhere.
2. Remove mentions of "Packages starting at" — we don't have tiers.
3. Clarify the Registered Agent wording to definitively state it does NOT auto-renew.
4. Focus the feature list purely on the Trio: Formation, EIN, Operating Agreement.
5. Replace "AI-generated documents" with "Automated state-specific templates."
