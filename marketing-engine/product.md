# SOSFiler Product Positioning

## Elevator Pitch
SOSFiler is the only LLC formation tool that gives you everything you need to start your business—formation, EIN, and operating agreement—for a flat $49. No hidden fees, no auto-renewing subscriptions, and no bullshit. 

## Value Propositions
1. **The End of the "$0 LLC" Scam:** Competitors lure you in with a free formation, then charge $200 for an EIN and sneak in $199/yr compliance subscriptions. We charge exactly $49.
2. **Everything You Actually Need:** You can't open a bank account without an EIN and an Operating Agreement. We include them by default.
3. **No Subscription Traps:** We don't automatically enroll you in registered agent services or legal consults. When you pay $49, you are done paying us.
4. **Fast & Accurate:** We are a precision utility. We file your state paperwork correctly the first time.

## Feature List (The $49 Package)
- State Articles of Organization Filing

## Positioning Statement
For first-time founders and serial entrepreneurs tired of being nickel-and-dimed, SOSFiler provides complete business formation for a flat $49 fee. Unlike LegalZoom or ZenBusiness, we never use subscription traps or withhold essential documents behind paywalls.
