# Content & Writing Rules

## 1. Formatting Standards
- **Headings:** Use clear, benefit-driven H2s and H3s. No clever puns.
- **Paragraphs:** Keep them short (3-4 sentences max). Use bullet points whenever listing features or benefits.
- **Bold Text:** Use bolding to emphasize exactly what the user gets and the exact price.

## 2. Required Elements in All Copy
Every landing page or major content asset MUST include:
1. **The Exact Price:** $49. Mentioned clearly above the fold.
2. **The "What's Included" Trio:** Always explicitly list Formation, EIN, and Operating Agreement.
3. **The Anti-Subscription Promise:** Explicitly state "No auto-renewals" or "No subscription traps."

## 3. The Forbidden Words
If you use these words, the copy fails QA:
- Solutions
- Leverage
- Empower
- Seamless
- Cutting-edge
- Innovative
- Synergize
- "Peace of mind"
- "Our team of experts"

## 4. Voice and Tone Rules
- **Be Direct:** "We file your LLC for $49." not "Our comprehensive business formation solutions help you get started."
- **Call Out the Industry:** Don't be afraid to mention the "$0 LLC" scam. Contrast our honest pricing with competitor tricks.
- **Never Sound Like a Law Firm:** We are software that files forms. We do not offer legal advice.

## 5. SEO Guidelines
- **Primary Keywords:** Focus on high-intent, long-tail phrases (e.g., "LLC formation without subscriptions", "Flat fee LLC filing", "How to get an LLC and EIN").
- **Meta Descriptions:** Must include the $49 price and the "no hidden fees" promise. Keep under 155 characters.
- **Content Structure:** Always answer the user's question directly in the first paragraph before expanding. No recipe-blog style fluff.
